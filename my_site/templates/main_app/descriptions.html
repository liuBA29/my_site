Mantra Player is a handy application designed for yoga studios or individual use. It is good for people who practice mantras and want to repeat them a specific number of times.

Why is it convenient?

Many people use different methods to count mantra repetitions, but with Mantra Player, everything becomes much easier and more enjoyable.

How does it work?

  • Select a mantra: Upload an audio file with the mantra in .mp3 or .wav format.
  • Set the number of repetitions: Enter the desired number of repetitions for the mantra to play.
  • Enjoy: Hit "Play," and the app will start playing the mantra for you, with the option to pause, resume, or stop at any time.

Who is it for?

Yoga Studios: Helps yoga instructors create a calm atmosphere with repeating mantras without being distracted by tracking the number of repetitions.

Individual Practice: Whether you're practicing at home or anywhere else, the app will be your trusty assistant during meditation or mantra repetition.

It is for free!
Download Mantra Player and try it for yourself. The app will become an indispensable tool in your practice.

This version is specifically designed for Windows operating system.
